Frequently Asked Questions

Is RustPress really free?

Yes! RustPress Core will be completely free. The free version includes all essential features to build fully functional websites. Paid tiers add premium themes, plugins, and support.

What's the difference between plans?

Starter ($9/mo) adds premium themes and basic plugins. Pro ($19/mo) unlocks all premium content and commercial license. Business ($49/mo) adds team collaboration. Enterprise offers custom solutions.

When will RustPress be available?

Starter, Pro, and Business plans launch in Q2 2026 for early access subscribers. Enterprise plans will be available in Q3 2026. Join our waiting list to get 30% off at launch!

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Absolutely. Start with any plan and upgrade or downgrade whenever you need. Your content and settings will be preserved when switching plans.

Do I need a paid plan to use RustPress?

No. The free version works completely standalone. Paid plans are optional and only needed if you want access to premium themes, plugins, team features, and priority support.

Is my data safe?

Yes. RustPress runs entirely on your own server or computer. Your data never leaves your control. We don't collect or store any of your content regardless of which plan you choose.
