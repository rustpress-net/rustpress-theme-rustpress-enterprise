What Are RustPress Themes?

RustPress themes are complete design packages that control how your website looks and feels. They include templates, styles, and layouts that work seamlessly with the RustPress CMS.

  • Tera Templates - Powerful templating with inheritance and macros
  • CSS Variables - Easily customize colors, fonts, and spacing
  • Responsive Design - Mobile-first layouts that look great everywhere
  • SEO Optimized - Structured data, meta tags, and fast loading
  • Accessibility - WCAG 2.1 compliant for all users
