Frequently Asked Questions

What is RustPress?

RustPress is a modern, high-performance content management system built with Rust. It combines the speed and safety of Rust with an intuitive interface for content creators.

Is RustPress free to use?

Yes! RustPress is open-source and free to use. We also offer enterprise plans with additional features and support for larger organizations.

How do I get technical support?

You can get help through our Discord community, GitHub issues, or by contacting us directly via email for enterprise support.

Can I contribute to RustPress?

Absolutely! We welcome contributions from the community. Check out our GitHub repository for contribution guidelines and open issues.
