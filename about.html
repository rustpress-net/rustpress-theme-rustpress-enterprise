About RustPress

Building the Future of Content Management

RustPress is a next-generation CMS built with Rust, designed for developers who demand performance, security, and flexibility without compromise.

Our Mission

We believe content management should be fast, secure, and enjoyable. Traditional CMS platforms are slow, vulnerable, and frustrating to use. RustPress changes that.

By leveraging Rust's performance and safety guarantees, we've created a CMS that's 10x faster than PHP alternatives while eliminating entire classes of security vulnerabilities.

The Story

RustPress started as a side project in 2024, born from frustration with existing CMS platforms. Why should a simple blog post take 500ms to render? Why are SQL injection vulnerabilities still making headlines in 2024?

We knew there had to be a better way. Rust offered the perfect foundation: blazing-fast performance, memory safety without garbage collection, and a developer community that values correctness.

After months of development, RustPress emerged as a full-featured CMS that proves you don't have to sacrifice usability for performance, or security for flexibility.

Today, RustPress powers thousands of websites around the world, from personal blogs to enterprise platforms. And we're just getting started.
